## ðŸŽ¬ Recording Tips:

### Camera Setup:
- **Clean background** (or blur it)
- **Good lighting** (face the window or use a lamp)
- **Eye level** (stack some books under laptop if needed)

### Audio:
- **Quiet room** (turn off fans, close windows)
- **Use AirPods** if you have them (better mic than laptop)
- **Speak clearly** but naturally

### Pacing:
- **Breathe!** Don't rush
- **Pause between sections** (easier to edit)
- **If you mess up** - just pause, take a breath, and restart that sentence
- **Smile when you talk** - it shows in your voice

### Screen Recording:
- **Use QuickTime** or **OBS Studio**
- **Hide your dock** (Command + Option + D)
- **Close extra tabs/apps** (clean desktop)
- **Full screen the browser** (Command + Control + F)
