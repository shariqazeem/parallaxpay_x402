## üé• RECORDING TIPS

### **Before Recording:**
1. Clear browser cache and cookies
2. Close all other tabs/windows
3. Use Incognito/Private mode for clean recording
4. Have wallet already connected with some SOL
5. Test the full flow 3 times before recording
6. Ensure at least 1 Parallax provider is showing as online
7. Use 1920x1080 resolution
8. Record with Loom or OBS at 1080p

### **During Recording:**
1. **Speak clearly and confidently** - no "um" or "uh"
2. **Move mouse smoothly** - no frantic clicking
3. **Pause after each section** - easier to edit
4. **Time yourself** - practice to stay under 3 minutes
5. **Show, don't just tell** - let the UI do the work
6. **No typos on screen** - judges notice everything

### **If Something Goes Wrong:**
- **Don't restart immediately** - pause, collect yourself
- **Worst case:** Record in 3 separate takes (Oracle, Marketplace+Analytics, Builder) then edit together
- **Audio issues:** Record video first, then do voice-over separately
